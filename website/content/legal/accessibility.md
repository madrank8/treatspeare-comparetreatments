## Our Commitment

[COMPANY LEGAL NAME], which operates Treatibly (treatibly.com), is committed to making the Site accessible to the widest possible audience, including people with disabilities. ## Conformance Goal

We aim to conform to the **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 and 2.2, Level AA**, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, and neurological disabilities.

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we treat WCAG 2.1/2.2 Level AA as a continuing target rather than a one-time achievement.

## Measures We Take

To support accessibility, we work to:

- structure content with clear, logical headings and meaningful page organization;
- provide text alternatives for meaningful images and non-text content;
- support keyboard navigation for interactive elements;
- use sufficient color contrast and avoid relying on color alone to convey information;
- write clear, descriptive link text and labels;
- design layouts that adapt to different screen sizes and that support browser zoom and text resizing; and
- consider accessibility when adding new content and features.
