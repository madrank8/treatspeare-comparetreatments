## 2. What Are Cookies and Tracking Technologies?

A **cookie** is a small text file that a website places on your device to store information, such as your preferences or an identifier. Cookies may be **session cookies** (deleted when you close your browser) or **persistent cookies** (remaining until they expire or are deleted).

We and our partners also use related technologies, including:

- **Pixels and tags** — tiny pieces of code that record that a page was viewed or an action was taken;
- **Local storage** — browser storage used to remember settings or data; and
- **Software development kits (SDKs) and similar tools** used for analytics and advertising.

In this Policy, "cookies" refers to all of these technologies.
