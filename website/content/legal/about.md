## Who We Are

Treatsphere (treatsphere.com) is an independent online publisher that reviews, compares, and ranks telehealth brands across the United States. The Site is owned and operated by Madrank Digital Ltd, a company organized under the laws of the State of Israel.

We cover the telehealth categories that matter most to everyday consumers — including weight management and GLP-1 medications, men's health, women's health, erectile dysfunction, testosterone and hormone therapy, hair loss, skin care, sexual health, and at-home blood testing.

## Our Mission

The telehealth market has grown quickly, and it can be hard for consumers to tell good options from bad ones — or to understand what they are actually paying for. **Our mission is to help people make informed, confident decisions about their health by making telehealth options easier to understand and compare.** We do this by researching brands carefully, scoring them against consistent criteria, and explaining what we find in plain language.

## What We Do

- We research telehealth brands, including their clinical oversight, pricing, products, policies, and customer feedback.
- We score and rank brands using a consistent, published framework — see our **Editorial and Review Methodology**.
- We publish reviews, comparisons, and explainer content to help readers weigh their options.

## What We Do Not Do

We want to be clear about our limits. Treatsphere is **not** a healthcare provider, a medical practice, a pharmacy, or a telehealth platform. We do **not**:

- provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment;
- prescribe, dispense, compound, or sell any medication or treatment;
- facilitate medical consultations; or
- create a doctor–patient relationship with anyone.

Our content is informational and educational only. Decisions about your health should always be made with a licensed healthcare provider. Please read our **Medical Disclaimer**.

## How We Make Money

Treatsphere is free to use. We are funded through advertising and affiliate relationships: when readers click through to a telehealth brand from our Site and take certain actions, we may earn a commission or fee. This can include affiliate commissions, paid placement, and pay-per-click or pay-per-call compensation.

We disclose this clearly and in detail in our **Affiliate and Advertising Disclosure**, and a short disclosure also appears on every review and comparison page near the links it describes. We believe you should always know how a publisher is funded.

## Editorial Independence

We earn money from some of the brands we write about — and we have built our process specifically so that this does not corrupt our reviews. **Compensation does not buy a better editorial score.** Scores are determined by the weighted criteria in our Editorial and Review Methodology, paid placements are clearly labeled, and editorial scoring is kept separate from commercial relationships. We do not publish fake or incentivized reviews, and we do not suppress honest negative feedback.

## Our Commitment to Accuracy and Trust

We hold our content to high standards of experience, expertise, authoritativeness, and trustworthiness. That means:

- using a consistent, transparent, and published review methodology;
- fact-checking key claims, especially pricing and clinical claims, before publication;
- being careful never to describe compounded medications as FDA-approved or as equivalent to FDA-approved brand-name drugs;
- dating our content and updating it as brands change; and
- correcting mistakes promptly — see the corrections policy in our Editorial and Review Methodology.
